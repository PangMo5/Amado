Amado is a companion app for the free Amado macOS menu bar agent. The companion requires macOS 15 or later and does not require an account or purchase.

Review steps:
1. On the iPhone setup screen, tap Get Amado for Mac or visit https://pangmo5.dev/Amado/.
2. Install and open the Mac app.
3. Choose Show pairing code in the Amado menu bar menu.
4. In the iPhone app, tap Scan pairing QR and scan the code.
5. Tap the paired Mac to lock it.

The one-tap lock action is available in the iPhone app, Apple Watch app, Home Screen widget, and Control Center. Bluetooth proximity auto-lock is configured and performed by the Mac app, so it does not require the iPhone app to remain open.
